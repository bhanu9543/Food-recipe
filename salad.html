Recipe of Chicken Salad

To make a delicious chicken salad, start by cooking boneless, skinless chicken breasts until fully cooked and then shred or chop them into bite-sized pieces. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken with diced celery, red onion, and halved grapes for a burst of sweetness. Add a generous amount of mayonnaise, a dollop of Dijon mustard for tanginess, and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of paprika for depth. Mix everything together gently until well combined. For extra crunch, toss in some chopped walnuts or pecans if desired. Serve the chicken salad chilled on a bed of lettuce or in sandwiches with whole grain bread. It's a refreshing and satisfying dish perfect for any occasion!
